About Us

Scented Crafts was first established April 21, 2024 currently based at San Isidro, San Fernando, Pampanga, currently our operations covers the city of San Fernando. One of the main reasons we got into the Perfume business is because of our strong passion and growing knowledge about perfume, We wanted to develop products that have an irresistable scents. Another reasons is for the benefits it has on people, the social aspect of having an excellent fragrance is important in social gatherings. People can now enjoy premium fragrance in such affordable prices. The wide variety of perfumes will guarantee that there is one that will fit you the most. We are glad that we are able to help people come together, through a whiff of perfume.
